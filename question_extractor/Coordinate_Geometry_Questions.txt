# ICSE Coordinate Geometry Question Bank

## ICSE Board Paper 2016

Q2(a) [3 marks] (easy)
    The slope of a line joining P(6, k) and Q(1 – 3k, 3) is 1/2. Find:
    (i) k
    (ii) Midpoint of PQ, using the value of ‘k’ found in (i).
    Subtopic: Slope and Midpoint Formula
    [Source: ICSE Board Paper 2016]

Q8(c) [4 marks] (medium)
    (i) Since, A lies on the x-axis, let the coordinates of A be (x, 0). Since B lies on the y-axis, let the coordinates of B be (0, y). P(4, -1) divides segment AB in the ratio 1:2. Find the coordinates of A and B.
    (ii) Find the equation of the line perpendicular to AB and passing through P.

## ICSE Board Paper 2017

Q3(b) [3 marks] (easy)
    In what ratio is the line segment joining P(5, 3) and Q(-5, 3) divided by the y-axis? Also, find the coordinates of the point of intersection.
    Subtopic: Section Formula (Ratio by Axis)
    [Source: ICSE Board Paper 2017]

Q7(b) [6 marks] (medium)
    Points A(0, 4), B(2, 3), C(1, 1) and D(2, 0) are vertices of a quadrilateral.
    (i) Plot these points on a graph paper and join them in order.
    (ii) Reflect quadrilateral ABCD on the y-axis and name the image as A'B'C'D'.
    (iii) Write the coordinates of A', B', C' and D'.
    (iv) State the name of the figure ABA'B'. (Note: B' is image of B)
    Subtopic: Reflection
    [Source: ICSE Board Paper 2017]

Q9(b) [4 marks] (medium)
    The vertices of triangle ABC are A(-1, 3), B(4, 2) and C(3, -2).
    (i) Find the coordinates of the centroid G of triangle ABC.
    (ii) Find the equation of the line through G and parallel to AC.
    Subtopic: Centroid and Equation of a Straight Line (Parallel Lines)
    [Source: ICSE Board Paper 2017]

Q5(c) [4 marks] (medium)
    Use graph paper for this question (Take 2 cm = 1 unit along both x and y axis). ABCD is a quadrilateral whose vertices are A(2, 2), B(2, -2), C(0, -1) and D(0, 1).
    (i) Reflect quadrilateral ABCD on the y-axis and name it as A'B'CD.
    (ii) Write down the coordinates of A' and B'.
    (iii) Name two points which are invariant under the above reflection.
    (iv) Name the polygon A'B'CD.
    Subtopic: Reflection
    [Source: ICSE Board Paper 2018]

Q7(c) [4 marks] (medium)
    A(2, 5), B(-1, 2) and C(5, 8) are the vertices of a triangle ABC. 'M' is a point on AB such that AM : MB = 1 : 2. Find the co-ordinates of 'M'. Hence find the equation of the line passing through the points C and M.
    Subtopic: Section Formula and Equation of a Straight Line
    [Source: ICSE Board Paper 2018]
