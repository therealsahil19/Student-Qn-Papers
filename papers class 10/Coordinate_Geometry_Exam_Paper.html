Question 1. Choose the correct answer from the options given below:

(i) ASSERTION (A): Line x = 3 is perpendicular to the x-axis.
REASON (R): Any line parallel to the y-axis is perpendicular to the x-axis.

(a) Both A and R are true and R is the correct explanation of A.

(b) Both A and R are true but R is not the correct explanation of A.

(c) A is true but R is false.

(d) A is false but R is true.

(ii) The inclination of line with equation √2x − √6y − 3 = 0 is:

(a) 30°     (b) 45°     (c) 60°     (d) 90°

(iii) Points A(x, y), B(3, −2) and C(4, −5) are collinear. The value of y in terms of x is:

(a) 3x − 11     (b) 11 − 3x     (c) 3x − 7     (d) 7 − 3x

(iv) Statement 1: The point which is equidistant from three non-collinear points D, E and F is the circumcentre of the ΔDEF.
Statement 2: The incentre of a triangle is the point where the bisector of the angles intersects.

(a) Both the statements are true.

(b) Both the statements are false.

(c) Statement 1 is true, and Statement 2 is false.

(d) Statement 1 is false, and Statement 2 is true.

(v) Assertion: Point A is reflected in y-axis to get point B, which is reflected in origin to get point C and Point C is reflected in y = 0 to get point P.
Reason: The point which will coincide with point P will be Point A.

(a) Both Assertion and Reason are true and Reason is the correct explanation of Assertion.

(b) Both Assertion and Reason are true but Reason is not the correct explanation of Assertion.

(c) Assertion is true but Reason is false.

(d) Assertion is false but Reason is true.

(vi) Assertion(A): The point (−2, 8) is invariant under reflection in line x = −2.
Reason(R): If a point has its x-coordinate 0, it is invariant under reflection in both axes.

(a) Both A and R are correct, and R is the correct explanation for A.

(b) Both A and R are correct, and R is not the correct explanation for A.

(c) A is true, but R is false.

(d) Both A and R are true.

(vii) Which of the following equation represents a line equally inclined to the axes?

(a) 2x − 3y + 7 = 0     (b) x − y = 7     (c) x = 7     (d) y = −7

(viii) Assertion: The points A(−1, 0), B(3, 1), C(2, 2) and D(−2, 1) are vertices of a parallelogram.
Reason: The co-ordinates of midpoint of both the diagonals AC and BD are (½, 1).

(a) Both Assertion and Reason are false.

(b) Assertion is true but Reason is false.

(c) Both Assertion and Reason are true but Reason is not the correct explanation of Assertion.

(d) Both Assertion and Reason are true and Reason is the correct explanation of assertion.

(ix) Which of the following is the slope of a line perpendicular to AB if A(−3, 2) and B(6, 8)?

(a) 3/2     (b) −3/2     (c) −2/3     (d) 2/3

(x) The reflection of point (3, −5) in the line x = m is point (−5, −5), the value of m is:

(a) 3     (b) −5     (c) −1     (d) 4
