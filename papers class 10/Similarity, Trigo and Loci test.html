Question 1
Choose the correct answers to the questions given below. Write the correct option in each case.
(i) The locus of a point P such that $\angle APB = 90^\circ$ for a fixed line segment AB is: [1]
(a) A line parallel to AB
(b) The circle with AB as diameter
(c) The perpendicular bisector of AB
(d) A pair of lines perpendicular to AB
(ii) If the volumes of two similar solids are in the ratio $64 : 125$, then the ratio of their total surface areas is: [1]
(a) $4 : 5$
(b) $8 : 11.18$
(c) $16 : 25$
(d) $64 : 125$
(iii) If $\tan \theta = \frac{5}{12}$, then the value of $\frac{\sin \theta + \cos \theta}{\sin \theta - \cos \theta}$ is: [1]
(a) $-\frac{17}{7}$
(b) $\frac{17}{7}$
(c) $\frac{7}{17}$
(d) $-\frac{7}{17}$
(iv) For points A and B, angle APB forms a right angle, then the locus of point P is: [1]
(a) A square with points A and B
(b) The circle with diameter AB
(c) A rectangle with side A and B
(d) A semicircle with diameter AB
(v) The three vertices of a scalene triangle are always equidistant from a fixed point. The point is: [1]
(a) Orthocentre
(b) Incentre
(c) Circumcentre
(d) Centroid
(vi) If the length of the shadow of a tree is decreasing then the angle of elevation is: [1]
(a) Increasing
(b) Decreasing
(c) Remains the same
(d) First increases and then decreases
(vii) In $\triangle ABC$, $DE \parallel BC$ with $AD : DB = 2 : 3$. If the area of $\triangle ADE = 8$ cm², then the area of trapezium DECB is: [1]
(a) 21 cm²
(b) 42 cm²
(c) 50 cm²
(d) 117 cm²
(viii) The number of points equidistant from three given non-collinear points is: [1]
(a) Zero
(b) One
(c) Two
(d) Infinite
(ix) $(\sec A + \tan A)(1 - \sin A)$ simplifies to: [1]
(a) $\sec A$
(b) $\sin A$
(c) $\cos A$
(d) $\text{cosec } A$
(x) A model of a building is constructed to a scale of $1 : 200$. If the volume of the model is $0.25$ m³, the volume of the actual building is: [1]
(a) 50,000 m³
(b) 1,000,000 m³
(c) 2,000,000 m³
(d) 500 m³
